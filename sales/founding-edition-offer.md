# IRL Editions Founding Edition Pilot

## The Offer

IRL Editions builds custom branded minting apps for communities, events, and brands that want people to leave a real-world moment with something memorable, collectible, and easy to claim.

The Founding Edition Pilot is a scoped 60-90 day launch package for one campaign, event, or community moment. It is designed for early partners who want a polished IRL minting experience now, while helping fund the continued development of IRL Editions.

## What You Get

- Branded camera and minting app
- QR claim flow for simple handoffs
- Giftable wallets for low-friction onboarding
- Community gallery for the campaign
- One event or campaign setup
- Backend and admin setup
- Hosting and support during the pilot
- Post-pilot recap
- Option to continue after the pilot

## Best Fit

This is for teams with a real audience and a real moment coming up:

- Communities launching a gathering, drop, or membership moment
- Events that want a more durable artifact than a photo booth
- Brands looking for a creative bridge between physical attention and digital ownership
- Artists, venues, and cultural teams that want the story of a night to keep circulating afterward

## Pricing

- Starting at $10k
- Typical founding pilots: $10k-$20k, depending on scope

Pricing depends on campaign complexity, visual customization, launch timeline, and any extra operational needs.

Ongoing hosting, support, light updates, or event concierge can be added after the pilot based on what the partner needs.

## Why Now

IRL Editions is aiming to close three pre-sale clients to fund continued product development.

Founding clients get a closer build relationship, more influence on the roadmap, and a launch partner who is directly invested in making the campaign work in the real world.

## Founder-Led Promise

This is not a generic SaaS package with a logo swapped in.

The goal is to make each pilot feel like it belongs to the community or brand using it: clear enough for guests, expressive enough to be remembered, and technically solid enough to trust during the moment.
